About Us

Enjoy a hassle-free shopping experience with services from Cake's 22 Bakery.

Unique cakes are crafted like a masterpiece, loaded with happiness.

Cake's 22 Bakery has founded in 2023. We prioritize service excellence and our customers' needs by building the most effective delivery and customer service team to achieve our goal of becoming the most reliable one-click-away-service online cake shop in Jakarta, Indonesia.
